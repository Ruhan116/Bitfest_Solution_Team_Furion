Quick & Easy Ramen

Ingredients:

1 package ramen noodles
1 tablespoon olive oil or vegetable oil
1/4 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup carrots, chopped
1/4 cup mushrooms, sliced
1/4 cup green onions, chopped
Soy sauce to taste
Sriracha to taste
Soft-boiled egg (optional)
Instructions:

Cook Noodles: Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add ramen noodles and cook according to package directions.
Sauté Veggies: While noodles cook, heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
Add Veggies: Add carrots and mushrooms to the skillet and cook until tender-crisp, about 5-7 minutes.
Combine: Drain noodles and add them to the skillet with the vegetables.
Season: Pour in ramen broth and add soy sauce to taste.
Top & Serve: Garnish with green onions, sriracha, and a soft-boiled egg (if using). Serve immediately.
